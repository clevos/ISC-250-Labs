African Grey Lifespan

  • 40-60 years as pets
  • Outside captivity lifespan is shorter by a-lot

Diet

  • Fruit
  • seeds
  • bird pellets

Relationship

  • Clones human speech well
  • May experience behavior issues if not used to the person
  • Very smart
  • Shares meals with other birds

Housing

  • Cage minimal 2 foot deep by 3 feet wide by 4 feet high
  • It has playpen top with a dish
  • They also always need to be flying around out of the cage
