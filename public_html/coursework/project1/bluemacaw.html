Blue and Yellow Macaw Parrot

  • Life span 30-35 years
  • Blue and Yellow Macow is a South American Parrot
  • Usually the top of the head is blue and the body gold with orange under tones, their beaks are black as well as under their chin and their feet are black colored
  • The birds can reach approxmently 30 to 34 inches and weigh two three pounds
  • Blue and Yellow Macow are endangered

Diet

  • Healthy Macow will consume around 10-15% of it's own body weight daily
  • Seeds
  • Different Plants
  • fruits
  • nuts

Housing

  • Cage must be minimum at least 5 feet tall and at least 3 or 4 foot wide and long

Training

  • Blue and Yellow Parrot needs 15 to 20 minutes of training a day
  • Ignore the bird negative behavior
  • Be expressive
  • Speak softly
  • Place bird in cage or perch

Temperament

  • They are socialable, but have to warm up to strangers
  • Intelligent and have a willingness to learn
